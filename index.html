About Me

Hello! My name is Sarah and I originally grew up in the tiny mountain town of NC.
In my early childhood my passions included reading, sewing, photography and dog agility training with my Dog Cali. My continued reading as a child grew and developed into a desire to travel and see the places I had only see through the pages of my books. Through a love of art and photography I went on to study entertainment technology so that I could be a part of bringing these stories to life.
Since my graduation, I have traveled to 17+ different countries for work, and have worked with individuals from all walks of life and cultural backgrounds. My love helping artists achieve their vision through programming of entertainment technology (concert tour lights, automation etc.) introduced me to an entirely different world of coding.
Over the years I have developed an increasing interest in immersive and interactive art as an educational and business engagement tool. The types of projects I now desire to work on require more than just a base knowledge of coding and computers, as I would like to create interactive applications which help further business engagement as well as make education/training more engaging and immersive.
